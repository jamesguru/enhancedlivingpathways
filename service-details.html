Support Coordination Service

Expert guidance to navigate your NDIS plan, connect with quality providers, and build a support team that helps you achieve your goals and live the life you choose.

Your Partner in Navigating the NDIS

Support coordination is a capacity building support designed to help you implement all supports in your NDIS plan, including informal, mainstream, community, and funded supports.

Your support coordinator works with you to understand your plan, connect you with providers, negotiate services and prices, and build your ability to exercise choice and control.

Plan Understanding

We help you understand your NDIS plan, budgets, and what supports you can access.

Provider Connections

We connect you with quality, registered providers who match your needs and preferences.

Service Agreements

We assist with service agreements, negotiations, and ensuring you get value for your funding.

Crisis Resolution

We provide support during service breakdowns or crises to ensure continuity of care.

Our Support Coordination Process

We follow a structured yet flexible approach that puts you at the center of every decision and action.

01
Initial Assessment & Goal Setting

We meet with you to understand your goals, preferences, and current situation. We review your NDIS plan together.

02
Support Design & Provider Matching

We design a support approach and connect you with providers that align with your needs, culture, and location.

03
Implementation & Coordination

We help establish services, negotiate agreements, and ensure all supports work together effectively.

04
Review & Capacity Building

We regularly review progress, build your skills to manage supports, and prepare for plan reviews.

NDIS Funding & Levels

NDIS Support Category Capacity Building
  • Support Coordination Level 1: For straightforward plans
  • Support Coordination Level 2: For complex support needs
  • Specialist Support Coordination: For high complex situations
  • Funded through your NDIS plan

*Must be included in your NDIS plan to access this support
