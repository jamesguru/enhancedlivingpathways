Social and Community Participation

At Enhanced Living Pathways, we support participants to actively engage in their community, build meaningful connections, and participate in activities that promote confidence, wellbeing, and independence. Our approach is flexible, person-centred, and guided by individual interests and goals.

What We Provide

  • Support attending social, recreational, and community activities
  • Assistance building friendships and social confidence
  • Assistance accessing education, volunteering, or skill-building opportunities
  • Support exploring hobbies and interests
  • Transport to and from community activities
  • Support to develop social skills and communication

Our Commitment

We are committed to supporting inclusion, choice, and empowerment, helping participants connect with their community, develop confidence, and enjoy a fulfilling, active lifestyle.

NDIS Funding Categories:
  • Community Participation (Core Supports)
  • Social and Community Participation (Capacity Building)

Mentoring

At Enhanced Living Pathways, our mentoring service supports participants to build confidence, develop life skills, and work toward personal goals in a supportive and encouraging environment. We focus on strengths, independence, and meaningful community participation.

What We Provide

  • One-on-one mentoring tailored to personal goals and interests
  • Support building confidence, motivation, and decision-making skills
  • Guidance with social skills, communication, and relationships
  • Encouragement to engage in community, education, or employment pathways
  • Practical strategies to promote independence and wellbeing
  • Goal-setting and progress tracking

Our Approach

We provide respectful, person-centred mentoring that empowers individuals to grow, build resilience, and achieve greater independence while feeling supported every step of the way.

Benefits of Mentoring:
  • Increased self-confidence and self-esteem
  • Better decision-making abilities
  • Improved social connections
  • Greater independence in daily life

Assistance with Daily Living and Household Tasks

At Enhanced Living Pathways, we provide practical, respectful support to help people manage everyday tasks while maintaining independence, comfort, and wellbeing at home. Our approach is person-centred, flexible, and tailored to participant routines, preferences, and goals.

What We Provide

  • Personal care assistance
  • Meal preparation
  • Household tasks including cleaning, laundry, and organisation
  • Assistance with shopping, appointments, and daily routines
  • Skill-building to promote independence in everyday living
  • Medication reminders and support

Our Commitment

We focus on dignity, choice, and empowerment, supporting you to live comfortably in your home, maintain independence, and build confidence in daily life.

NDIS Funding Categories:
  • Assistance with Daily Living (Core Supports)

Psychosocial Recovery Coaching

Our Psychosocial Recovery Coaching service supports people living with mental health challenges to work toward recovery, independence, and meaningful participation in everyday life. We take a trauma-informed, strengths-based and person-centred approach that values your lived experience, goals, and aspirations.

What We Provide

  • Recovery-focused coaching tailored to your personal goals
  • Support building daily living skills, resilience, and confidence
  • Coordination with mental health services, carers, and supports
  • Strategies for managing mental health challenges
  • Support to build and maintain social connections
  • Assistance with navigating mental health systems

Our Approach

We walk alongside you in your recovery journey, promoting independence, wellbeing, and connection. Our coaches are trained in trauma-informed practice and understand the unique challenges of psychosocial disability.

NDIS Funding Category:
  • Psychosocial Recovery Coach (Capacity Building)

Support Coordination

At Enhanced Living Pathways, our Support Coordination service helps participants understand, navigate, and make the most of their supports. We work alongside you to build confidence, strengthen independence, and ensure your services align with your goals and needs.

What We Provide

  • Guidance to understand your NDIS plan and funding
  • Connection to suitable service providers and community supports
  • Assistance coordinating multiple services effectively
  • Support to build skills for greater independence and choice
  • Advocacy to ensure your needs and preferences are heard
  • Preparation for plan reviews

Our Commitment

We focus on empowering you with knowledge, choice, and control, helping you access the right supports.

Support Coordination Levels:
  • Level 1: Support Connection
  • Level 2: Coordination of Supports
  • Level 3: Specialist Support Coordination

Supported Independent Living

At Enhanced Living Pathways, we provide supportive accommodation options designed to help participants live as independently and confidently as possible. Our services are person-centred, flexible, and focused on building everyday living skills while ensuring comfort, stability, and wellbeing.

Short-Term Accommodation (STA) / Respite

We offer short stays in a welcoming and supportive environment. This can provide a break for participants and their carers, opportunities to try new experiences, or transitional support when needed.

  • Flexible stay durations
  • Fully accessible accommodation
  • 24/7 support available
  • Social and recreational activities

Medium-Term Accommodation (MTA)

Our medium-term housing options provide stable accommodation while longer-term housing solutions are being arranged. We ensure continuity of support and comfort during this transition period.

  • Up to 90 days accommodation
  • Bridge to permanent housing
  • Support to find long-term solutions
  • Skill development for independent living

Supported Independent Living (SIL)

Our Supported Independent Living (SIL) services assist participants to live as independently as possible in a supported environment with shared or individual supports.

  • 24/7 support options
  • Shared or individual living
  • Life skills development
  • Community integration
