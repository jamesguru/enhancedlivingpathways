What We Provide

  • • Support attending social, recreational, and community activities
  • • Assistance building friendships and social confidence
  • • Assistance accessing education, volunteering, or skill-building opportunities

Supported Independent Living

At Enhanced Living Pathways, we provide supportive accommodation options designed to help participants live as independently and confidently as possible. Our services are person-centred, flexible, and focused on building everyday living skills while ensuring comfort, stability, and wellbeing.

Short-Term Accommodation (STA) / Respite: We offer short stays in a welcoming and supportive environment. This can provide a break for participants and their carers, opportunities to try new experiences, or transitional support when needed.

Medium-Term Accommodation (MTA): Our medium-term housing options provide stable accommodation while longer-term housing solutions are being arranged. We ensure continuity of support and comfort during this transition period.

Supported Independent Living (SIL): Our Supported Independent Living (SIL) services assist participants to live as independently as possible in a supported environment.
