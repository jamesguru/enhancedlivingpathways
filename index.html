Understanding the NDIS

National Disability Insurance Scheme

Your Plan, Your Choices

The NDIS provides funding to eligible people with disability to gain more time with family and friends, greater independence, access to new skills, jobs, or volunteering in their community, and an improved quality of life.

The NDIS is designed to help people with disability achieve their goals. Whether it's finding and keeping a job, making friends, or becoming more independent, the NDIS can provide the support you need to live the life you want.

Your NDIS plan is unique to you, based on your individual goals, needs, and aspirations. We help you understand your plan, connect with the right supports, and make the most of your NDIS funding.
